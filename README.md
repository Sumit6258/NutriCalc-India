## 🇮🇳 The Problem

Most nutrition calculators are built around **Western food databases.**
They don't know what Dal is. They don't understand Poha. They've never heard of Upma.

The result? **Millions of Indian users guessing their calorie intake** with tools that simply aren't built for their diet.

<br/>

<div align="center">

| ❌ What most apps offer | ✅ What NutriCalc India offers |
|:---:|:---:|
| "Flatbread" instead of Roti | **Roti with accurate macros** |
| Generic "lentil soup" | **Dal, the real thing** |
| No Idli, no Dosa, no Sabzi | **Full Indian meal support** |
| Built for the West | **Built for India 🇮🇳** |

</div>
